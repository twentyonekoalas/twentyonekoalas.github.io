Creation For Conservation

Wildefolk is a project focused on bringing together musicians, visual creators and environmentalists lead by the popular YouTube brand Koala Kontrol.

Our goal is to cultivate and share the work of talented creators who share our passion for environmental sustainability and animal welfare.

About Us

With roots in the Pacific Northwest, Wildefolk are individuals, collaborators, and supporters of the world around us. We promote the message of Creation for Conservation and it’s our goal to share audio visual experiences with people all over the world. Listening to a song, watching a video or sharing our message allows us to raise the necessary funds we use to fund amazing environmental projects we believe make an impact.

By sharing Wildefolk and Wildefolk creators, you are doing your part to become a contributor for environmental conversation.
